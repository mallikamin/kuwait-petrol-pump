# Petrol Pumps.docx - Extracted Requirements

## Core Objectives
1. Digitize petrol pump operations end-to-end
2. Minimize manual data entry using OCR and automation
3. Integrate sales, purchases, and expenses into unified system
4. Enable real-time reporting and monitoring of Sales/Customer balances etc
5. Provide seamless integration with QuickBooks Online
6. Support scalability for multiple petrol pump locations

## Major Components
1. Mobile application with built-in POS, OCR scanning of Dispensing units' meter (Liters sold), online ordering all integrated with POS itself
2. Single POS System for both fuel and non-fuel items
3. Inventory Management System
4. QB Online Integration
5. Admin Dashboard & Reporting
6. Multi-Branch Management

## POS System - Fuel Sales Workflow

### Step 1: Meter Reading via Mobile App
- Pump staff capture images of dispensing unit meters (separately for PMG and HSD nozzle wise)
- OCR will scan and read meter readings automatically
- Data captured:
  * Date & time
  * Pump number / dispensing unit
  * Fuel type (PMG / HSD or Nozzle number)
  * Meter reading value

### Step 2: Automatic Sales Calculation
- System stores last shift closing reading
- Current reading will be subtracted with last closing
- Separate calculations for PMG (Petrol) and HSD (Diesel)
- System automatically applies current fuel rates

### Step 3: Sales Bifurcation Screen
- After automatic calculation, accountant/manager allocates total sales into payment types:
  * Cash
  * Credit
  * Bank Card
  * PSO Card (or other fuel cards)

**Data Capture During Bifurcation:**
- For Cash Sales: Option to mark as walk-in customer
- For Credit Sales:
  * Customer name
  * Vehicle number
  * Slip number
- Bifurcation must be done separately for HSD and PMG
- System validation: Total bifurcation must match total calculated sales

## General POS Features
- Option of Built-in POS within application
- Real-time transaction recording
- Shift-based operations (opening & closing)
- Operator login and tracking

## Non-Fuel Sales
- Sales of items: Engine oil, Oil filters, Other retail items
- Features:
  * Item-wise billing
  * Inventory deduction
  * Separate reporting from fuel sales

## Requirements
- Fast and responsive interface
- **Offline capability with sync when internet is restored** (CRITICAL)
- Hardware compatibility (receipt printer, barcode scanner if needed)
