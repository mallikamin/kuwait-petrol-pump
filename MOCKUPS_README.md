## ❓ Questions to Answer

Before picking a theme, consider:

### **Geography**
- Where are primary users? (Pakistan, Kuwait, International)
- Need multi-language support? (English, Urdu, Arabic)
- Time zones with night shifts?

### **User Types**
- Tech-savvy or traditional?
- First-time users or experienced?
- All roles (cashier, operator, accountant) or specific?

### **Brand Positioning**
- Modern/tech-forward or traditional/trusted?
- Premium or efficient/value?
- Local/community or international?

### **Accessibility**
- Night shift support needed? (→ Dark theme)
- Color-blind users? (→ Avoid red-only warnings)
- Older users? (→ Larger fonts, higher contrast)
