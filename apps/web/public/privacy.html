Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Kuwait Petrol Pump POS ("we", "our", "the System") is a point-of-sale application designed for petrol pump operations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our system.

2. Information We Collect

The System collects and processes the following types of information:

  • User Information: Names, roles (cashier, manager, admin), and authentication credentials
  • Business Data: Sales transactions, fuel meter readings, inventory levels, customer information, and product details
  • Financial Data: Transaction amounts, payment methods, and QuickBooks Online sync data
  • System Logs: Usage logs, error reports, and audit trails for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and record fuel sales transactions
  • Manage inventory and product catalog
  • Synchronize financial data with QuickBooks Online
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Maintain system security and prevent fraud
  • Provide customer support

4. QuickBooks Integration

When you connect the System to QuickBooks Online:

  • We request read and write access to your QuickBooks company data
  • Sales transactions are automatically synced to QuickBooks as invoices
  • Customer and product information is synchronized between systems
  • We securely store QuickBooks OAuth tokens using industry-standard encryption
  • You can revoke QuickBooks access at any time through the System settings or your QuickBooks company settings

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • QuickBooks tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Role-based access control limits data access to authorized users
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Daily automated backups of all system data

6. Data Retention

We retain your business data for as long as your account is active. Transaction records and financial data are retained for a minimum of 7 years to comply with accounting and tax regulations.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties except:

  • QuickBooks Online: Only the data you authorize for synchronization
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the System
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Revoke QuickBooks integration at any time

9. Cookies and Tracking

The System uses essential cookies for authentication (JWT tokens) and session management. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

The System is designed for business use and is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the System after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
